Sparcify

Basics

Sparcify is a web application that finds the best neighborhood for your night based on gender ratios. You can also view the people in the neighborhood and post messages. I worked on a team with three others to make this web app.

My Role

While two team members worked on the back-end of Sparcify, I worked on the front-end with another person. While she wrote the Angular side of things, I wrote the HTML and CSS to fit in with that. As the UI developer of the team I considered our team's vision of the app and UI and UX principles when developing the design and code for Sparcify. From beginning to end I saw through the design of the app and made sure everything looked good only four days after starting for Demo day!

Technologies

We worked in a Node environment utilizing an MVC framework, Mongo DB, and Grunt. The front-end was developed with Angular JS, HTML, and CSS. I worked within the views components to build a dynamic app using semanticly meaningful HTML, a responsive grid system, and modularized, reusable CSS. As the site would be mainly used on mobile phones, I optimized the experience for mobile users and developed the site keeping progressive enhancement a top priority. I also used Adobe Photoshop, Icomoon Fonts, and Style Tiles when developing the look and feel of the site.
